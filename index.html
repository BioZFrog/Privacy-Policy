Privacy Policy

Quran-e-Majeed — Mujtaba Creates
Effective Date: February 22, 2026
This Privacy Policy applies to the Quran-e-Majeed app developed by Mujtaba Creates. It explains how we handle information when you use our app. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being fully transparent.

1. Location Access
We request location access solely to calculate accurate prayer times. Your location coordinates are used only for this purpose and are sent to the Aladhan API to retrieve prayer times. We do not store your location on any external server, and it is never used for tracking, profiling, or any other purpose.

2. Data Collection
We do not collect personal information such as your name or email address. The app only stores your last-read Surah and verse locally on your device for your convenience. No personal data is transmitted to us or any third party beyond what is described in this policy.

3. Third-Party Services
Our app uses the following trusted third-party services:

Aladhan API (aladhan.com)
Used to fetch prayer times based on your location. Your approximate location coordinates are sent to their servers solely for this purpose, without storing any identity information.
Arabic Text
IndoPak Quran Text from typemybook.com (originally from InPage Quran Publishing Software), verified by Ayman Siddiqui and R. Siddiqua for QuranWBW.com and Quran.com. Made for Sadaqa-e-Jaria purposes.
Font — IndoPak Nastaleeq
From the Quranic Universal Library, created by Ayman Siddiqui and R. Siddiqua.
qul.tarteel.ai/resources/font/242
Translation — Al Bilal Muhammad et al (2018)
Public Domain — Unlicense.
github.com/fawazahmed0/quran-api

Note: The translation is one interpretation of the meanings of the Quran. For detailed understanding, please consult scholars and multiple translations.

4. Children's Privacy
This app is safe for all age groups and does not collect personal data from anyone, including children under the age of 13. If we become aware that any such data has been collected, we will promptly take steps to delete it.

5. Security
We protect your privacy by never storing data on any external server. Your data stays on your device and is never shared. While no system can guarantee 100% security, we take all reasonable measures to ensure your information remains safe.

6. Offline Content
All Surahs and translations are either bundled with the app or downloaded once for offline use, ensuring secure and reliable access to the Quran without requiring a continuous internet connection.

7. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. We recommend reviewing this policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
